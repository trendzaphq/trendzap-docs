# Quick Start

Get started with TrendZap in just 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave)
- A wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet)
- ETH on Arbitrum for gas fees
- USDC on Arbitrum for betting

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [trendzap.xyz](https://trendzap.xyz)
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection

:::tip
If you're new to Arbitrum, you'll need to add it to your wallet:
- **Network Name:** Arbitrum One
- **RPC URL:** https://arb1.arbitrum.io/rpc
- **Chain ID:** 42161
- **Currency Symbol:** ETH
:::

## Step 2: Get Arbitrum ETH

You need ETH on Arbitrum for gas fees. Options:

### Bridge from Ethereum
1. Visit the [Arbitrum Bridge](https://bridge.arbitrum.io)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Enter amount and bridge

### Buy Directly
Purchase ETH directly on Arbitrum through:
- Coinbase
- Binance
- Most major exchanges

## Step 3: Get USDC

TrendZap uses USDC for betting. Get it by:

1. **Bridging** - Bridge USDC from Ethereum
2. **Swapping** - Swap ETH for USDC on Uniswap
3. **Buying** - Purchase USDC on a CEX and withdraw to Arbitrum

## Step 4: Place Your First Bet

1. **Browse Markets** - See active prediction markets
2. **Pick a Market** - Click on one that interests you
3. **Choose Side** - Select OVER or UNDER
4. **Enter Amount** - How much USDC to bet
5. **Confirm** - Approve the transaction in your wallet

🎉 **Congratulations!** You've placed your first TrendZap bet!
