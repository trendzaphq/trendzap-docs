# What is TrendZap?

TrendZap is the first prediction market built specifically for social media virality. You bet **USDC** on whether a post will hit an engagement threshold — views, likes, retweets, comments — before a deadline. When the market closes, the oracle checks the actual metric, the outcome is settled on-chain, and winners claim automatically.

No intermediaries. No manual resolution. No custody.

---

## The core idea

Every day, posts go viral. Some you predicted. Most you didn't bet on.

TrendZap changes that. For any public post on X (Twitter) or YouTube, you can now take a position:

- **OVER** — the post will exceed the threshold
- **UNDER** — it won't

Markets resolve automatically when the deadline passes. The oracle reads the real metric, the smart contract determines the outcome, and winners claim from the pool.

---

## Who it's for

### Predictors
You have strong opinions about what goes viral. Now you can back them. Browse live markets, pick your side, and win USDC if you're right.

### Market Creators
Anyone can create a market. Paste a post URL, set a threshold and deadline, add a seed bet, and you're live. You earn a **3% fee** from the total pool when the market resolves.

### DeFi users
On-chain prediction markets with LMSR pricing, instant settlement, and no KYC. Everything verifiable on [Snowtrace](https://snowtrace.io).
