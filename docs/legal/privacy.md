# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: April 5, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how TrendZap ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our application at [app.trendzap.xyz](https://app.trendzap.xyz) or our documentation at [docs.trendzap.xyz](https://docs.trendzap.xyz).

---

## 1. Our Approach

TrendZap is a non-custodial, permissionless protocol. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, or verify your identity to use the App. This is by design.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information you provide

- **Wallet address** — When you connect a wallet, your public blockchain address is used to identify your positions, transactions, and winnings. This address is public by nature of the blockchain.
- **Market creation inputs** — If you create a market, the post URL and threshold you provide are stored on-chain (public) and in our database for display purposes.

### Information collected automatically

- **Usage data** — We may collect anonymised data about how the App is used (page views, feature interactions) to improve the user experience. This does not include personal identifiers.
- **Blockchain data** — All transactions executed through TrendZap are permanently recorded on the Avalanche C-Chain and are publicly accessible by anyone.

### Information we do NOT collect

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses (unless voluntarily provided for support)
- Government-issued IDs or KYC documents
- Private keys or seed phrases — **never share these with anyone, including us**

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Display your market positions, bet history, and claimable winnings
- Operate the leaderboard and user profile features
- Debug issues and improve the Protocol
- Ensure the security and integrity of the platform

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. On-Chain Data

All on-chain activity (bets, market creation, claims, resolutions) is recorded permanently on the Avalanche C-Chain. This data is:

- **Public** — visible to anyone with access to the blockchain
- **Permanent** — cannot be deleted or modified by TrendZap or by you
- **Pseudonymous** — tied to your wallet address, not your real-world identity

---

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and local storage for:

- Remembering your recent searches and preferences
- Session management (wallet connection state)
- Basic analytics (anonymised, aggregate only)

You can clear local storage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. Some features may not function correctly without them.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

TrendZap integrates with third-party services to function. These services have their own privacy policies:

- **Privy** — wallet connection and embedded wallet management ([privy.io/privacy](https://privy.io/privacy))
- **Avalanche Network** — on-chain settlement and data
- **Social platforms** — post embed previews are fetched via official platform APIs; we do not store the content of social posts

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain off-chain market metadata (titles, thumbnails, descriptions) for as long as the platform operates. If you would like data associated with your wallet address removed from our off-chain database, contact us at **privacy@trendzap.xyz**.

Note: we cannot remove data that has been written to the blockchain.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

TrendZap is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has used the App, please contact us.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

---

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: **privacy@trendzap.xyz**
